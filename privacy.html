4. Data Storage & Security

4.1. Meeting recordings are processed and not retained on our servers after processing. Generated documents are stored exclusively in your Google Drive.

4.2. Account and metadata are stored on encrypted servers with the following security measures:

  • TLS 1.2+ encryption for all data in transit
  • OAuth 2.0 authentication (no password storage)
  • Access controls and audit logging
  • Regular security reviews

5. Third-Party Data Processors

We share data with the following categories of third-party processors, each bound by data processing agreements:

6. International Data Transfers

Some of our processors are located outside the EU/EEA. Where applicable, we ensure adequate protection through Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or reliance on adequacy decisions.
